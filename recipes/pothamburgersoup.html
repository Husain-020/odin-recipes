Pot Hamburger Soup Recipe

Description

In this recipe we will be creating a hamburger in a coup! In the form of a soup.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Any type of seasoning(s)
  • Any type of meat(s)
  • Any time of vegitable(s)

Steps

  1. Put water in a boil
  2. Add your chosen meat(s)
  3. Add your chosen vegitable(s)
  4. Add your chosen seasonings
  5. Mix everything while the water is boiling
  6. Let soup cooldown
  7. Server in a cup!
